WEEK 1 [23 March 2020 - 29 March 2020]

Kick off meeting was conducted through Skype. The meeting started with self-introduction from us and our client. We have proposed our team’s profile which includes respective member’s role and personal insights. A presentation slide prepared by client was shared to us, regarding project requirements and tools to be used throughout the implementation of the project. We have proposed multiple questions to seek for client’s reply. It was a fruitful session as we got our queries answered.


WEEK 2 [30 March 2020 - 5 April 2020]

Went through client requirements thoroughly. Research was conducted on how to achieve given requirements. I have tried out the websites given by the client to us as reference. Cheqroom is an equipment management application that helps to perform fast and secure equipment management. The features found in Cheqroom are very similar to our project requirements. I was able to visualize the look of our application and how the respective function works.


WEEK 3 [6 April 2020 - 12 April 2020]

Informal meeting was conducted through Discord with full attendance. One of the meeting agenda was to discuss project group charter’s content. Ground rules and team goals were set and agreed by all team members. Conducted discussion on A1 documentation content. Tasks to be done were listed out and were segregated evenly to all members. Few wireframe tools were proposed in the meeting, Adobe XD, sketch and Figma. We had our eyes on Figma as it is a web-based prototyping tool with real time collaboration. It allows multiplayer collaboration and the interface can be shared to every single member within a group. Final decision was made on which tool to use and Figma appeared to be the one.


WEEK 4 [13 April 2020 - 19 April 2020]

Meeting was held in the morning through Discord with full attendance. A1 documentation was reviewed to check on everyone’s progress. Majority of the work has been done and everyone may proceed to work on each remaining task. We have outlined the functions to be included in the wireframe. I have never tried, don’t even know how to build a wireframe, but this time I would like to give it a try.


WEEK 5 [20 April 2020 - 26 April 2020]

An informal meeting was held through Discord with full attendance. In the meeting, we had checked on individual’s work progress before compilation. Minority of the tasks were left to be done 3 days before the due. Later, we conducted discussion on the wireframe’s outlook and its functions. We have gone through multiple websites found online to seek for wireframe’s design inspiration. Since I am taking the lead in wireframe design and implementation, it is a must for me understand the operation of the Figma application before taking real actions. Hence, I have scrolled through related websites and viewed few Youtube videos covering Figma introduction and tutorial. As a beginner, I found it difficult to use and it slowed down my working progress. After trying variations of layout and their arrangement, I am getting familiar to it and eventually speeds up the design process. Feel good to learn something new!


WEEK 6 [27 April 2020 - 3 May 2020]

An informal meeting was held through Discord. We have checked on every member’s work before compilation and submission. We have decided to attach two different schedules in the report, where Gantt Chart is referring to the project’s progress while team’s timeline is referring to our group’s working schedule. Since I have drafted a wireframe dashboard, it was shared via screen sharing to everyone for feedback and comments. So far, my members are ok with the design, just that I have to change some layout and icons. Further discussion has been made on wireframe functionality and layout. I have conducted research on how to improve the wireframe’s look and feel.


WEEK 7 [4 May 2020 - 10 May 2020]

More research has been conducted on wireframe’s functionality design. It is important to prioritize the mandatory functions and outline good to have functions in the wireframe. The wireframe design will reflect on how our actual program will look like. My concern was on how to express the functions in the wireframe. The main concept for wireframe design is to improve efficiency and accessibility. I have tried to make the important functions interlink with each other, for example when the user clicked on A to B, and to go to C, the page will redirect the user to C from B. The user does not haveto go back to the main page to do so. Basically, in this whole week, I have focused on wireframe design and working towards its completion.

I took quite a long time explaining it as the functionalities were a lot. Then there goes the Q&A session where Ms Robina asked a few questions on some specific functionalities and proposed ideas to us to improve it. Overall, I feel that the presentation was quite ok, for me, I have to slow down my speech next time.

WEEK 11 [1 June 2020 - 7 June 2020]

Continue to conduct research on gathered requirements. Meanwhile, the wireframe has been modified according to newly added requirements. I have added extra features into reservation and checkout process, aimed to simplify the processes, to improve efficiency as well as to gather user feedback upon checkout. The existing maintenance process worked differently from the actual operation, so I have proposed a sequential task execution format that explains the whole maintenance operation in the wireframe. The concept of such design is to simplify the maintenance handling process and allow new staffs to understand the whole process easily.

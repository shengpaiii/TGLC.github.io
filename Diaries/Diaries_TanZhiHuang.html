WEEK 1 [23 March 2020 - 29 March 2020]

First week of our final year project, since we had already chosen the project about software for online monitoring and management system for factory and hospital equipment with IoT technology, before having the kick-off meeting with our clients Mr. Bernard Yong and Mr. Ng from Obsnap Instruments Sdn Bhd, we had already did some discussions about how we should do and what questions we needed to ask. During the kick-off meeting, they had explained to us more clearly about what we need to do and what are the objectives and goals of this project. Besides, we also asked them some questions we felt confused and not so understand during Q&A period. This meeting is helpful to us because after this meeting we had gotten the ideas about how the system will look like and also clear our doubts in some parts of the project.


WEEK 2 [30 March 2020 - 5 April 2020]

In this week, I had done some research about the IoT and learnt the tools that need to be used to do the project. We also had a group meeting to discuss about project topic. We finalized to mainly focus about the equipment management system part, and then secondary focus on IoT and Data Centre part. Besides, we had did our project workflow list (Gantt Chart). Based on the Gantt Chart we can know that in which time we need to process until where. If all the work can be done on time according to the Gantt Chart, then this project can be delivered to clients on time.


WEEK 3 [6 April 2020 - 12 April 2020]

We had an informal group meeting to discuss about the project group charter, A1 documentation tasks, and the tools used to do wireframe. We had discussed and set up our group’s rules and goals that agreed by every group member. After that, we had assigned the A1 documentation tasks to each group member to complete. The tasks are divided evenly to every member. Before end of meeting, a few wireframe tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, Figma and etc. were suggested by group members. We had discussed each tool’s pros and cons and final decided to use Figma to be our wireframe tool.

We had our formal meeting (checkpoint 1) in Tuesday 4 – 5 pm. There was a small issue occurred before the meeting started which was one of our client Mr. Ng’s skype had problem to use (we supposed to use skype to do this meeting). They informed me 10 minutes before the meeting start, so at that moment I needed to quickly find another solution to handle this problem which was changed the meeting tool from skype to discord. I had communicated with Ms. Robina and all my group members that we needed to use discord to do this meeting. Luckily all of them had used discord and were willing to change the meeting tool to discord and the meeting could be started on time.
The important things that we presented were the tools and development environment we wanted to use, wireframe, use case diagram, and UML diagram. All these could be considered as our prototype design draft and ideas of the system. Each group member had covered different parts during presentation, and I was presented use case diagram, UML diagram and our action plan. Our clients had asked few questions while we were presenting, luckily they accepted majority of our design and ideas. During the Q&A section, Ms. Robina had also suggested few ideas and functions that could improve the system to clients and us. For those ideas and functions that agreed and accepted by clients, we were willing to change or add it to our system in order to make the system become more perfect.
After the meeting ended, I had sent our presentation slides to clients to review and also a UOW Short Form Confidentiality Agreement to them and wanted them took a look at it. If there was no issue, one of them needed to sign their signature and sent it back to me.
The presentation slides (A2 documentation) together with UOW Short Form Confidentiality Agreement were submitted to Moodle on 13th May 2020.

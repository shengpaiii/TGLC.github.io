ABOUT US

We’re TGLC, a team of 4 Bachelor of Computer Science (University of Wollongong Program) students from INTI International College Subang (IICS), who work tirelessly to deliver results that contribute to a positive return on investment. Our creative ethos is rooted in our clients need driving ideas that excite and inspire.

"Veni, Vidi, Vici." – Julius Caesar

//Mission

Being professional software development team. Our mission is to serve our customers to the best by delivering technically sound and affordable business solutions while ensuring exceptional customer support throughout the process.

//Vision

We believe technology can make life better for everyone if used in the right way. Hence, we want to deliver the best of our IT services using the latest technology that will drive our clients' business towards growth.
